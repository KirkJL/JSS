HOW TO PLAY

🗺️ The Mountain

You lead a tribe stranded atop a procedurally generated mountain. The world is hostile — storms, floods, and freezing cold will test you every season.

👥 Your Tribe

Your people need Food, Shelter, and Warmth. Neglect any for too long and they die. Keep all three up and they multiply.

⛏️ Resources

Tap tiles to gather Wood (🌲), Stone (🪨), and Food (🌿). Resources are finite — plan carefully.

🏗️ Buildings

Use the BUILD menu to place structures. Shelters protect your people. Storage expands capacity. Watchtowers give disaster warnings.

⛈️ Disasters

Storms, floods, and blizzards will strike. A Watchtower warns you 1 turn early. Survivors who have shelter take less damage.

📅 Time

Each tap of END TURN advances one day. Seasons change every 30 days — winter is deadly. There is no win, only survival.
